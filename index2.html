How to play

There are 2 players, player X and player O. Player X goes first. X clicks on an empty space in the grid to activate it, then clicks on the X to the right to place the X in the selected empty space. Then player O clicks an empty space in the grid to activate it and then clicks on O to place an O in the selected empty space. The first player to fill in 3 spaces in a row, vertically, horizontally or diagonally wins! If neither player is able to fill in 3 spaces in a row, the game is a draw and both players start again.
